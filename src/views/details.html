Model: Bugatti Chiron Super Sport 300+

Posted by: Alex Smith

Manufacturer: Bugatti

Engine Type: 8.0L Quad-Turbo W16

Top Speed: 304.8 kmph

Description: The Bugatti Chiron Super Sport 300+ is a record-breaking hypercar that became the first production vehicle to break the 300 mph barrier. With a sleek aerodynamic design and unmatched performance, it's powered by a monstrous 8.0-liter quad-turbocharged W16 engine producing 1,577 horsepower. Only a few were made, making it one of the rarest and most sought-after cars in the world.
